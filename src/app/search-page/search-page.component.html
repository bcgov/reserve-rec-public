Book a day-use pass

Where would you like to go?

Day-use passes are required at some popular BC Parks during the summer and winter seasons. Reserving a pass helps us manage the number of park visitors, when and where required, so we can protect the park and you can have a better visiting experience.

Detailed program information and history can be found on the day-use pass information page.

Book passes starting at 7:00 am PT two days before your visit. Refresh your browser at 7:00 am PT to see new passes available each day.
  • There are no first come, first served options upon arrival when passes are required.
  • There are a set number of passes available each day. Cancelled passes become available again, so check back frequently.
  • You may reserve one of each pass type (AM, PM, and ALL DAY) per day.
  • You cannot transfer your pass to another date, time, location, or person.
  • Once you select an available pass, you have 7 minutes to complete your  booking. After 7 minutes, the pass will be made available to others.
  • If passes are sold out, check again another day. We cannot provide passes via email or special request.
Vehicle passes

Vehicle passes are valid for up to 12 people (adults and children) travelling in the same vehicle.

Trail passes
  • Trail passes must be booked for each individual person, per day.
  • A maximum of 4 trail passes may be booked per reservation.
  • Children 12 and under accompanied by an adult do not require a trail pass.
